Where to buy

  • An Initial Public Offering occurs when a private company issues stock to the public for the first time. IPO is an example of a primary market.
  • IPOs provide companies with an opportunity to obtain capital by offering shares through the primary market.
  • The secondary market is the stock market and refers to the New York Stock Exchange, the Nasdaq, and other exchanges worldwide.
  • The secondary market is where investors buy and sell securities they already own.
