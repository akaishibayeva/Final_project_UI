Benefits

  • Predictable income stream
  • Have less volatility than stocks
  • Pay higher interest rates than savings account
  • Repayment of principal - you get all your money back at maturity

Risks

  • Default risk - the issuer can't pay the interest or principal in a timely manner or at all
  • Inflation risk - the rate of price increases in the economy deteriorates the returns associated with the bond
  • Liquidity risk - investors may have difficulty finding a buyer when they want to sell and may be forced to sell at a significant discount to market value
